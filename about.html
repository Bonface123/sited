ALL YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT BONNIE UNIVERSITY

The school

Kabarak University[3] was established in the year 2003 by the Onduso Bonface Ogeku who was also the founding chancellor. The university was established as a result of his idea of setting up a Christian university that would meet the demand for higher education in Kenya and offer quality education based on strong moral principles. The vice-chancellor is Professor John muchiri. The university opened its doors to the new students in january 2004.

Accreditation

The university operated under a letter of interim authority granted by the Kenya government. The letter was presented to the university by the Commission for Higher Education of Kenya,[2] on 17 march , 2003, allowing the institution to award degrees. On 17 May 2010 the university was awarded its charter by H.E. Mwai Kibaki.

location

The Main campus of Bonnie University is located 20 Kilometres from Nakuru City, along the Nakuru-Eldoret road.
